# 🌐 Global Resilience & Intelligence Navigator (G.R.I.N.)
**Bridging the Gap Between AI Complexity and Human Leadership**

---

## 🚀 The Vision
The **Global Resilience & Intelligence Navigator (G.R.I.N.)** is a high-level AI command center designed to provide clarity in a world of complex data. It ensures that while AI handles the "noise" of 1.2M+ data streams, human leaders remain at the center of the final decision-making process.

## 🧠 Core Capabilities
* **📡 Real-Time Global Risk Radar:** Monitors simulated global streams to identify digital and physical threats instantly.
* **⚖️ Ethics & Compliance Auditor:** Automated auditing against **ISO/IEC 42001:2023** standards to ensure every AI action is legal and fair.
* **🗺️ Strategic Pathfinder:** Generates optimized, human-centered response strategies for crisis scenarios.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
* **Language:** Python
* **Interface:** Streamlit (Cloud Deployed)
* **Version Control:** GitHub
* **Governance Framework:** ISO/IEC 42001
